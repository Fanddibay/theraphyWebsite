About

Let's do this together! Meet the Founder

Welcome to TFL! As a therapist, I’m privileged to work with diverse clients and hear their stories. I specialize in anxiety, OCD, trauma, relationships, sports, and professional performance. I also offer life coaching, mental toughness training, and emotional support animal letters. I work with individuals, couples, and families, focusing on young adults, students, and adults. My approach is holistic and collaborative, ensuring a safe, empathetic space to support your goals. I'm licensed in Alabama, Florida, Georgia, Indiana, Michigan, Ohio, and Virginia. Please note, I’m a boutique practice and do not accept insurance.
